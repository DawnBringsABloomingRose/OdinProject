Drunken Noodles

Description

Drunken noodles are a dish from Thailand, featuring a blend of spices and vegetables

Ingredients

  • Rice Noodles
  • Tofu
  • Soy sauce
  • Siracha
  • lime
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Cut the tofu into squares, marinade it with the soy sauce, siracha and lime
  2. Cut the onion and fry in a wok
  3. Add the tofu and tofu marinade to the wok
  4. Prepare rice noodles to package directions then add to the wok
  5. Garnish with fresh basil if you like
