Sweet Potato Po Boys

Description

A vegan twist on a classic lousiana dish

Ingredients

  • Sweet potato
  • French Bread
  • Coleslaw
  • Breading
  • Garlic Aolia
  • Fennel Marmalade

Steps

  1. Cut the sweet potato into discs, bake them at 400F for 20 minutes
  2. Once baked, bread them and then fry them
  3. Add everything to the french bread
